A growing set of deep-dive sandboxes — each one takes a single idea and lets you pull it apart, step by step, the way the Playground does for the quick intuitions. These are teaching sketches with real math (not trained models), built so you can watch the numbers move. More landing here over time.

  • July 15, 2026
    Sampling: temperature, top-p & top-k →

    Turn raw logits into a next-token distribution. Slide the temperature, carve out a nucleus with top-p, cap with top-k, and watch how the same model can be a sharp elitist or a playful roulette.

    Word embeddings & analogies →

    Meaning as geometry. Pick three words, do the vector math king − man + woman, and see which point the result lands on. A toy 2-D space where analogies are literally arithmetic.

    representationsgeometry
  • July 15, 2026
    Softmax & temperature, viscerally →

    The one function that turns logits into probabilities. Edit the logits, sweep the temperature, and feel why cold models are decisive and hot models are chaotic.

    mathintuition
  • July 15, 2026
    The attention matrix, laid bare →

    Type a sentence and watch it become a grid. Every cell is "how much does this word look at that word" — computed from real query·key dot products, hover any cell for the number.
