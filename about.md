I'm **Jiawei Cui** — a developer who spent years shipping software (networking, distributed systems) and is now going all-in on AI and machine learning.

This blog is where I think in public: deep dives into LLMs and the papers behind them, practical ML engineering, and the occasional build. Formerly writing code for a living; now training and shipping models.

## What you'll find here
- **Paper Deep-Dives** — close readings of foundational and frontier LLM papers: transformers, attention, Mixture-of-Experts, RLHF and DPO, reasoning, agents, and multimodal models.
- **Practical ML** — notes on training, fine-tuning (LoRA), embeddings, and retrieval.
- **Playground** — interactive, hands-on visualizations of the ideas above.
- **Projects and Engineering** — builds, tooling, and lessons from shipping software.
